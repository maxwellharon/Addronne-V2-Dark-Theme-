History and Background

Addrone Digital is a value addition company within the advertising space. We are a registered entity under the Kenyan company regulation act that was founded on April 30th 2018. Addrone Digital will offer advertising services to companies that are looking to employ drone marketing techniques as the cornerstone of their marketing program. With the base of operations in Nairobi, Kenya, Addrone Digital is a company which will approach the advertising industry with a focus on the utilization of drone technology. The company will spare no expense in using the very best and most versatile drones and cameras available on the market for acquiring statistics which benefit the client and ensure they reach their desired audience In today’s economy, this cost reduction is paramount to success. Addrone Digital service price range will be competitive and adjustable, depending on the client’s needs, while providing the highest quality advertisements to clients looking for personalized content. Addrone Digital's highly experienced and knowledgeable workforce will provide a brand-new set of eyes to the advertising industry that will allow them to bring a new, fresh and more exciting advertising technique which will guarantee to deliver on the client’s wishes and expectations. While providing up-close and carefully tailored services for each of our clients, and aiming to be one of the industry’s best, driven by passion and a strong will to succeed Addrone limited will have ushered a new of advertising in the region. As the business gradually grows, so will the client base and the workforce needed to service the clientele. This undertaking will begin its operations in Nairobi, Kenya, with the possibility of expanding its services to the neighboring areas in the future.

Fire Disaster

Addrone is involved with fire rescue services and uses it's drones to put out fires . The dones are fitted with special cameras that enable the drones to see through concrete walls and be able to identify the persons trapped in the building . The dones can also be equipped with fire extinguishers which can be used to put out fires.

Drone racing using VR Headsets

Addrone takes VR gaming to a whole new level. The VR racing uses the cameras positioned at a 360 degree angle all round the drone to enable the player to view the sorroundings of the drone through the VR Headset
